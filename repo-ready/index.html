Philosophy

One heat source.
Total control.

Every dish that leaves our kitchen has spent time over embers — grilled, smoked, roasted in ash, or licked by direct flame. We built the room around a single wood-fired hearth because limitation sharpens technique. Char is not a mistake here; it's a seasoning.
